Ways to Take Action

There are many simple ways to reduce waste without having to incorporate major changes in your lifestyle. From changing the type of lightbulb that you use to only buying food that you are going to finish, there are plenty of effortless ways to fulfill your role in stopping climate change. Additionally, you can go to volunteer opportunities that help the environment. Some events include tree plantings and holding educational events to teach other people about climate change. Another way to get involved is to sign petitions and to join protests. By raising awareness about climate change, we can get government leaders to take more steps toward a better future. Here are a few organizations that you can go to to learn about how to join the fight against climate change.
