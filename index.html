STOP CLIMATE CHANGE
We are the last generation that can save Earth.

Destruction of Animal Habitats

Many animals are losing their homes due to climate change. The increase in temperature is melting the ice in Antarctica and the Arctic at an alarming rate, which means that animals like walruses, polar bears, and penguins don’t have anywhere to live.

Natural Disasters

Due to climate change, there will be an increase in the amount of natural disasters. The rise in sea level will cause flooding in coastal regions. The increase in temperature due to greenhouse gases will cause droughts and stronger hurricanes. The dry soil and vegetation from the high temperatures will also cause more forest fires.

Effect on Humans

The changes in the environment will affect farmers everywhere. It will cause a disruption in their crops and if there isn’t an adequate food supply, there will be starvation. Due to the destruction of cities from all the natural disasters, millions of people will be left homeless.

Human Illnesses

All the pollution and toxic air can cause a variety of respiratory issues for people, especially for infants and the elderly. If someone is constantly exposed to pollution, then they can get lung cancer or emphysema.
